    Warranty Exclusions

    While we strive to cover every aspect of our service, certain limitations apply:

    • Natural wear and tear or deterioration over time.
    • Issues caused by improper use or external factors beyond the service provider's control.
    • Services that deviate from the original scope of work agreed upon at booking

    How to Claim Warranty

    If you face any issues with our service, follow these simple steps to claim your warranty:

    1. Contact us via phone, email, or live chat within 7 days of the service completion.
    2. Provide your booking details and a brief description of the issue.
    3. Our support team will review your claim and arrange for rework, repair, or compensation as applicable
